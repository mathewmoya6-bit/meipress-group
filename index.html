FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and processes.

How do I apply for a service?
Simply click the "Apply Now" button on this page, select the service you're interested in, fill out the application form, and submit it. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 24 hours.
Which countries do you support for international education?
We support applications to universities in USA, Canada, Australia, UK, Germany, and several other countries. We have partnerships with over 100 universities worldwide.
Do you offer education loans or financial support?
Yes! We offer guidance on education loans with no consignor requirements, scholarship opportunities, and financial planning assistance through our partnership with leading education finance institutions.
How long does visa processing take?
Visa processing times vary by country and type. Generally, student visas take 2-8 weeks, while work visas can take 1-3 months. We guide you through the entire process to ensure a smooth application.
How do employers recruit drivers through MEI Group?
Employers can contact us directly to discuss their driver recruitment needs. We conduct thorough screening, background checks, and defensive driving assessments to ensure we match you with qualified, reliable drivers.
