FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

How do I apply for a service?
Simply click the "Apply Now" button on any service card, select your service, fill out the form, and submit. Our team will respond within 24 hours.
Which countries do you support for international education?
We support applications to the USA, Canada, Australia, UK, Germany, and many other countries through our 100+ university partnerships.
Do you offer education loans or financial support?
Yes! We provide guidance on education loans with no consignor required, scholarships, and financial planning through our partner institutions.
How long does visa processing take?
Visa processing varies by country—typically 2-8 weeks for student visas and 1-3 months for work visas. We guide you through every step.
How do employers recruit drivers through MEI Group?
Employers can contact us directly. We conduct thorough screening, background checks, and defensive driving assessments to match you with qualified drivers.
What does Occupational Safety & Health training cover?
Our OSH training covers OSHA 2007 compliance, risk assessment, fire safety, first aid, PPE management, occupational health surveillance, chemical safety, permits to work, and emergency preparedness.
What driver license classes do you support?
We support all driver license classes including A2 (Light Motorcycle), A3 (TukTuk), B1 (Automatic), B2 (Manual), B3 (Professional), C1 (Light Truck), C (Heavy Truck), CD (Hazardous Material), CE (HGV), D1 (14 Seater), D2 (33 Seater), D3 (55-63 Seater), G (Industrial/Agricultural), and Classes 1-12.
